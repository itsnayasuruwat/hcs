# SK
Hi, Happy Birthday 🥰

Every word; Every touch

Affects me just a little too much

A smile from you gleams light from the divines

A slip of the tongue maims like a knife


Is this what it means to be in love?

Tossing pennies in the pond, praying you’re the one

Collecting tears in pools, pondering if I’m a fool

Simultaneously a white knight and a dark horse

Here is all of me; without a sliver of remorse

I plead you catch me before I fall

The one to end goodbyes once and for all


But if you walk away as a chapter and not an ending

I’ll still look for you at every party I’m attending

Because every word and every touch affects me just a little too much

I’m only crying because I’m In love
